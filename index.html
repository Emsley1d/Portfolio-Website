Asteroid-Belt

A Space-Invaders style game built solely by myself over the course of a week using; HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery.

Users can move a spaceship left and right across the screen whilst firing missles to destory approaching Asteroids. User input is controlled by keypress events.

Rate My Cat

A CRUD application built over the course of a week. This was a group project built using; HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Express (including CRUD and Authentication), API CRUD, Middleware, MongoDB and Mongoose.

Users can sign up to the website; upload photographs of their cats, rate other users cats and view a leaderboard of the current ratings.

NutriCO2

A CRUD application built over the course of a week. I worked on the basic functionality, the file/folder structure, user authentication and ultimately presented the project. This was a pair project built over the course of a week using; HTML, CSS, Python, JSON, JavaScript, Django and NoSQL.

User's can sign up to the website; upload photos of recipe ingredients; the photos are cthen onverted into text and the user provided with a list of Nutrional information and the CO2 emisions of the raw ingredients.

PokeGotchi

A fullstack MERN application built over the course of a week. I worked in a team of three and focused predominantly on the Front End; linking an external API with a Get ,request; the overall design and user interface and I also presented the project.

A Tamagotchi style app; users can sign up and select a Pokemon to become their 'PokeGotchi' pet. User's can then clean, feed, heal and play with their PokeGotchio to restore its HP (Hit Points).
